Mayflower Research Resources

  • New England Historic Genealogical Society — A fabulous website for New England research. Members have access to premier record databases. The library in Boston, Massachusetts holds one of the largest collections of New England genealogies, vital records, and manuscripts, staffed by well-trained specialists.
  • Pilgrim Hall Museum — The nation's oldest continuously operating museum. Learn more about its extraordinary collection of Pilgrim possessions and artifacts.
  • The Plymouth Colony Archive Project — University of Virginia project with primary source documents from Plymouth Colony. (Link may be inactive.)
  • General Society of Mayflower Descendants — The national organization for all Mayflower descendants.
  • Caleb Johnson's MayflowerHistory.com — Comprehensive online reference for Mayflower passengers, the voyage, and Plymouth Colony history.
