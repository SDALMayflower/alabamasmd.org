2022

November 19, 2022
Compact Day Commemoration & SMDAL 70th Anniversary Celebration
The American Village Trust • Montevallo, Shelby County, AL • 11:30 a.m. • $25.00 per person
Featured Speaker: Robert Scott Davis
The State Society held its business meeting, a buffet lunch, and an update from Dr. Erin Kate Scheopner, GSMD Director of Genealogy and Research Services. Robert S. Davis is a senior professor of History and director of the Genealogy Program at Wallace State Community College, Hanceville, AL. His publications include Tracing Your Alabama Past, Research In Georgia, Andersonville Civil War Prison, The 1833 Land Lottery of Georgia, and Georgia Citizens and Soldiers of the American Revolution.

2013

November 23, 2013 — 11:00 a.m.
State Society Meeting
The Club • Birmingham, AL • Board of Assistants 10:00 a.m.
Speaker: Governor General Bruce MacGunnigle — 32nd Governor General, General Society of Mayflower Descendants
A member for 40 years, Governor General MacGunnigle joined the Rhode Island Society at age 24. He is a descendant of William Brewster, James Chilton, Francis Cooke, Edward Fuller, Stephen Hopkins, Thomas Rogers, and Richard Warren, with twelve lines of descent to seven of the twenty-three Mayflower families.
