We Will Plymouth Rock You Weekend

The Society of Mayflower Descendants
in the State of (Sweet Home) Alabama

📅 Friday, April 24 & Saturday, April 25, 2026

Join Us for the "We Will Plymouth Rock You" Weekend!

As we approach our organization's 75th year in 2027, we are endeavoring to make 2026 one of the biggest years in our Society's history. To kick things off, we invite you to join us in Mobile, Alabama, for a landmark celebration during the last weekend of April.

Weekend Schedule

Friday Night: Welcome Dinner
Kick off the weekend with a social dinner at one of downtown Mobile's iconic restaurants. It is the perfect time to reconnect with old friends and meet fellow descendants.

Saturday Daytime: 2026 Spring Conference

  • Location: National Maritime Museum of the Gulf (situated right on the bank of the Mobile River).
  • Program: Member meeting, luncheon, and a keynote presentation.
  • Featured Speaker: Mr. Tom Begley, Executive Director of Plimoth-Patuxet Museums. As the head of the homeport for the Mayflower II and the 17th-century Pilgrim Village, Mr. Begley brings unparalleled expertise to our event.

Saturday Night: 40th Anniversary Gala
Finally, on Saturday night, we will be celebrating the local Gulf Coast Mayflower Colony's 40th Anniversary by having a Pilgrim Families Reunion.

Experience the Museum

Everyone who registers for and attends our conference will have an all-day pass to tour the National Maritime Museum of the Gulf (10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.). After our featured speaker is finished, everyone will still have two hours to tour the museum's plethora of exhibits.

Bring the Family: I strongly encourage you to bring your whole Mayflower-descended family, regardless of whether they are "official" members of the society. They are always welcome at our events.

Watch and Plan

Be sure to check out the videos and flyers on this page! The video highlights all the amazing things to see at the museum to help you plan your visit. We also encourage you to come a day or two early, or stay a day or two after. Make it a long weekend and enjoy all our area of the Gulf Coast has to offer.

Registration & Waitlist

We need an accurate headcount so we know how many attendees to plan for. You will be able to register and pay for the events starting on or before April 1st, but please get on the waitlist NOW.

How to Join the Waitlist:

  • Fill out the form below on this page.
  • OR email your name and email address to: governor@alabamasmd.org

No matter if you arrive by car, train, plane, ship, or horse-drawn wagon, just remember:
We Will Plymouth Rock You!

Kevin Sewell
Governor, Alabama Society of Mayflower Descendants
