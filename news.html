"We Will Plymouth Rock You" Weekend

The Society of Mayflower Descendants in the State of Alabama — April 24 & 25, 2026

Upcoming Event Posted March 2026

As we approach our Society's 75th Anniversary in 2027, we are determined to make 2026 a landmark year for the Alabama Society of Mayflower Descendants — and it begins this April in Mobile.

Friday, April 24 — Launch the weekend with a Welcome Dinner at Mobile's iconic T.P. Crockmier's restaurant in the heart of the downtown entertainment district.

Saturday, April 25 — Our 2026 Spring Conference at the National Maritime Museum of the Gulf on the Mobile River, featuring a members meeting, a luncheon, and our featured speaker Tom Begley, Executive Director of Plimoth Patuxent Museums in Plymouth, Massachusetts.

Make your plans now — this is one weekend you won't want to miss.
